Amanda is 18 and attends a small charter school. She's a California native and in her free time loves to take pictures, read, volunteer and code. Amanda wants to encourage the younger generation to explore all that can be done in the Computer Science world.

Alyssa Greenhalgh

Alyssa is going to be a junior at Lincoln high School. Never being exposed to coding she joined Girls Who Code this summer. She states that this experience has changed her views on computer science drastically. She also stated that knowing now that the career choice in computer science is leading significantly in males, she is hoping to challenge that fact and encourage other girls to rise up too and start coding. In her last years of high school she hopes to further her knowledge on computer science.
